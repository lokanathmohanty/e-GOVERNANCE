About the Portal

A unified digital platform transforming public service delivery.

Overview

The e-Governance Service Management System is a state-of-the-art digital infrastructure designed to bridge the gap between the government and its citizens. Initiated by the Department of IT & Electronics, this portal serves as a single-window interface for accessing over 50+ government services, ensuring transparency, accountability, and speed in public administration.

Built on robust, secure, and scalable technology, the portal empowers citizens to apply for certificates, pay taxes, register grievances, and track their applications in real-timeâ€”all from the comfort of their homes.
