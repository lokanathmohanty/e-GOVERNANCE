Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register on the portal? expand_more
Click on the "Login/Register" button on the top right corner. Select "Citizen Registration", fill in your details including Aadhaar number and mobile number, and verify with OTP to create your account.
What documents are needed for Income Certificate? expand_more
You typically need: 1. Identity Proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID), 2. Address Proof, 3. Salary Slip or Income Affidavit from Notary, and 4. Passport size photograph. Specific requirements may vary by district.
How can I track my application status? expand_more
Go to your Dashboard after logging in. You will see a list of all your applications with their current status (e.g., Pending, Approved, Rejected). You can also click on the application ID for a detailed timeline.
Is there a fee for applying online? expand_more
Some services are free, while others have a statutory government fee. The fee amount is displayed before final submission. Payments can be made via Net Banking, UPI, or Credit/Debit cards.
