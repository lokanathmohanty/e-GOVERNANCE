Common Questions

Click on the "Login" button on the top right corner. Select "Register", fill in your details including Aadhaar number and mobile number, and verify with OTP to create your account.

You typically need: 1. Identity Proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID), 2. Address Proof, 3. Salary Slip or Income Affidavit, and 4. Passport size photograph.

Go to your Dashboard after logging in. You will see a list of all your applications with their current status. You can also click on the application ID for a detailed timeline.

Some services are free, while others have a government fee. The fee amount is displayed before final submission. Payments can be made via UPI, Net Banking, or Cards.
