Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and represent the Government's commitment to protecting the personal information of citizens using this portal. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make a transaction.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide public services:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, Aadhaar Number, Date of Birth, Gender, Address.
  • Contact Information: Phone Number, Email Address.
  • Documents: Scanned copies of identity proofs, land records, or educational certificates uploaded for applications.
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, and access times for security auditing.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is utilized strictly for:

  • Processing applications for services you requested.
  • Verifying your identity against national databases (e.g., UIDAI).
  • Communicating status updates concerning your applications.
  • Generating statistical reports for administrative improvements (anonymized data only).

4. Data Security

We implement state-of-the-art security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption, secure data centers, and role-based access controls. Your data is stored on government-approved servers within the country and is protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies to maintain your login session. No third-party tracking cookies are used for advertising purposes.

6. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@odisha.gov.in
